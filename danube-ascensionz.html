/ OVERVIEW

A tower that reads as a single, rising gesture.

AscensionZ is conceived as a landmark residential tower for Dubai — a tall, slender volume whose facade is wrapped in continuous, undulating balcony bands that carry the eye upward and give the building a distinct silhouette against the skyline.

The sculpted balconies do more than shape the profile: they extend each apartment into a shaded outdoor room and vary the plan floor by floor, so no two levels read quite the same. At night the flowing lines are picked out in light, turning the tower into a beacon on the waterfront.
