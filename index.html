/ ABOUT

Between the drawing and the discourse.

Mohamed Rafik Sadek is an architect and urban planner whose practice sits between studio, classroom and research desk. His work spans competition design, digital fabrication and the study of cities under change.

He currently teaches at the College of Architecture and Digital Design, leading courses from architectural design and advanced CAD to sustainable envelopes and digital fabrication — and publishes on AI in pedagogy, adaptive facades and informal urbanism.
