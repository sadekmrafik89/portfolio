/ BIOGRAPHY

Sharing knowledge, shaping cities.

A dedicated and hard-working educator interested in exploring ways to help the environment by teaching students how to make urban public space and architecture more sustainable and environmentally sound. His passion is the design of buildings that create social, economic and political empowerment in urban areas.

His path spans studio, classroom and research desk — from earth-building and parametric-design workshops across Europe, India and Egypt to competition entries and urban-research initiatives such as the “Desert City Mappers” mapping of informal services in desert cities. Research interests include desert-city planning, sustainable urbanism, community development, public-space design, housing, environmental analysis and parametric design.
