A development read against the Aseer highlands.

Al Khuzama Land is an ADEC development proposal set in Aseer — the mountainous, green southwest of Saudi Arabia. The scheme is presented as an animated walkthrough that moves through the layout and its landscape setting.

This project came to us as video only, so the walkthrough above stands as its record for now. Send over any stills, plans or a written brief and I’ll fold them into the page in the site’s house style.
